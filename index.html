Instruction

Concentration, also know as Match,Memory, Pelmanism, Shikei-suijaku, Pexeso or simply Pairs, is a card game in which all of the card are laid face down on a surface and two card are flipped face up over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards.
